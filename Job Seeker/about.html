Our Story

Founded in 2018, Job Seeker began with a simple mission: to make job searching more transparent, efficient, and human-centered. We noticed that traditional job boards were frustrating for both job seekers and employers.

Job seekers struggled to find reliable information about company cultures and realistic salary expectations, while employers found it difficult to connect with genuinely interested and qualified candidates.

Today, we've grown into a comprehensive career platform that combines job listings with company reviews, salary data, and career resources - all designed to create better connections between people and opportunities.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Job Seeker free for job seekers?

Yes! Job Seeker is completely free for job seekers. You can search for jobs, read company reviews, access salary data, and apply for positions at no cost.

How does Job Seeker protect user privacy?

We take privacy seriously. We never sell your personal data to third parties. Company reviews can be posted anonymously, and we use encryption to protect all user information.

How accurate is the salary data?

Our salary data is sourced from verified user submissions, employer reports, and government databases. We regularly update and verify information to ensure accuracy.

Can companies respond to reviews?

Yes, registered employers can respond to reviews on their company page. We encourage constructive dialogue while maintaining our community guidelines.

How do I delete my account?

You can delete your account at any time from your account settings. Please note that anonymous reviews will remain to help other job seekers.

What makes Job Seeker different from other job sites?

We combine job listings with transparent company reviews and salary data, creating a more holistic platform for career decisions. Our focus is on quality connections, not just quantity.
